Microservices are powerful, but they bring complexity. Here are the key lessons learned from implementing them.

Essential patterns:

- Circuit Breaker
- Service Discovery
- API Gateway
- Event Sourcing

Observability is key:

- Distributed tracing
- Centralized logging
- Real-time metrics

Common challenges:

- Eventual consistency
- Resilience
- State management
- Coordinated deployment
